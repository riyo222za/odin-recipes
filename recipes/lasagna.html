Lasagna Recipe



Description

Lasagna is a dish that is best served piping hot and fresh. It is one of those comfort foods that you can never get tired of especially in the cold winter months, Make this dish to share with friends or just to meal prep for a few days and keep handy.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • tomato products
  • sugar
  • spices and seasonings
  • lasagna sheets
  • Cheases
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let lasgna rest before serving.
